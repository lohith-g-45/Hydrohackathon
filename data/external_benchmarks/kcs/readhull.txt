<Variable description>

all variables are in meters for full scale ships.

(body plan)
xlongi: distance from A.P.(stern rudder stock)
        (i.e., xlongi=0 at A.P., xlongi=Lpp at F.P.)
height: waterline height from base line
        (i.e., height=0 at keel line of midship)
breadth: half breadth at xlongi and height
        (i.e., breadth=0 at center plane)
inum: number of station, where offset points are given
jnum(i): number of points at a station

      read(12,'(a50)')shipname
      read(12,*)inum
      do i=1,inum
        read(12,*)xlongi(i),jnum(i)
        read(12,*)(breadth(i,j),j=1,jnum(i))
        read(12,*)(height(i,j),j=1,jnum(i))
      enddo

(centerline profile)
xprof: longitudinal position
       (i.e., xprof=0 at A.P,, xprof=Lpp at F,P,)
zprof: profile height from base line
iaf: number of centerline profile points

      read(13,*)iaf
      read(13,*)(xprof(i),i=1,iaf)
      read(13,*)(zprof(i),i=1,iaf)
